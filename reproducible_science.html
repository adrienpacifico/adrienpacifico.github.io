I'm a public economist and my research focus on taxation of households, inequalities of opportunity, and fertility.
I believe that the role of the economist is to provide insightfull analysis helping citizens to make informed public-decision.
To do so, i firmly believe that scientific results must be fully reproducible, and that the only way to achieve technical and practical reproducibility is to use free and open-source software (FOSS). To simulate the tax-benefit system I use OpenFisca which is a Python based microsimulation software. I also happen to have invested in technical tools that are quite trendy for Data-science application.
