Explore the French Income Tax with OpenFisca.

OpenFisca is an OpenSource microsimulation software that can be used to explore the French tax-benefit system. I try to explain how does the French income tax works in some of its dimensions. Simply click on the "launch binder" icon to start to explore the French income tax!
